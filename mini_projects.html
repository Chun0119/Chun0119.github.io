My Day in HKU


My Day in HKU is a RPG game built by HTML5 Phaser, which aims to help the new incoming University students to explore HKU. It is a leisure project that built by the Computer Science Association, Engineering Society, HKUSU, for promoting the society in the registration day of The University of Hong Kong.

Try the game: https://j.mp/gohku
