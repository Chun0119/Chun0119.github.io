VR Projects

抗毒小英雄VR is a local multiplayer VR game, which players have to team up and fight against the enemies. It aims to carry out anti-drug education where the enemies are representing drugs and players have to defeat them with team coorperation.

More Details: Eteam Portfolio


道路指揮家VR is a VR game which support single player and also multiplayer mode. Players will act as the police to direct the traffic. Players have to give out commands to pedestrians and drivers for controlling their movement. Besides, there will be some special events happened during the duty, players have to give out suitable warning to warn the pedestrians and drivers. Throughout the game, players may learn more about the rules of using the road according to the knowledge in "Road Users' Code" published by the Hong Kong SAR Government.

More Details: Eteam Portfolio


魔幻迷藏VR is a networked multiplayer VR game, and it is also a strategic survival game. One of the players will be the witch who tries to catch and imprison other players, the elves. And the elves are required to find the keys to open the locked door without being caught. An event was held to provide free game to the public.

More Details: Eteam Portfolio

Technical Components

Game Engine

Unity with C# language, Unreal with C++ language, SparkAR with JavaScript language are used.

Augmented Reality (AR)

ARFoundation, ARKit and Vuforia are used to provide the AR-related feature, such as image recognition and body tracking.

Virtual Reality (VR)

Oculus Quest and Oculus Go are used as the target devices for developing the VR games.

Mixed Reality (MR)

HoloLens 2 and Nreal are used as the target devices for developing the MR softwares.

Multiplayer Connection

AWS, Photon Cloud and Online Subsystem are used to provide the multiplayer connection server / services.

Body Tracking

Cubemos with Intel RealSense, Azure Kinect and ARKit are used to provide the body tracking service.

Voice Chat System

Vivox, Photon Voice Chat are used to provide the voice chat system service.

Other Input Devices

Arduino and bluetooth controller are used to receive input.
