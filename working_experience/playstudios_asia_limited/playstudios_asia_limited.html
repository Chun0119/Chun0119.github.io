myVEGAS Slots


myVEGAS Slots is a social casino slot game, which players have to spin the slot and earn chips. Throughout the gameplay, players will gain some points that can redempt for a real-world rewards.

In the game, there are features like daily missions, album, club, etc. The live operations of the game including the events, promotions and sales running per day.

Distribution Platforms: Apple App Store, Google Play, WebGL

Internal Tools
