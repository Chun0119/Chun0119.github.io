myVEGAS Slots


myVEGAS Slots is a social casino slot game in which players have to spin the slot and earn chips. Throughout the gameplay, players will gain points that can be redeemed for real-world rewards.

The game features daily missions, an album, a club, and more. The live operations of the game include events, promotions, and sales that run every day.

Distribution Platforms: Apple App Store, Google Play, WebGL
