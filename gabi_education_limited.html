Unity Software Developer (Part-time)

Gabi Education Limited is an organization that aims to promote educational equality through gamification of learning. I was responsible for building a series of applications to help potential dyslexia students learning Chinese. More details can be found in https://gabi.hk/dltd/.

  • built numbers of mobile educational applications with over 50 mini games included
  • built a server with database to store game data
  • built a web-based platform for game data management

Applications Introduction

The series of applications are having different purpose, for example, to carry out pre-screening of dyslexia, to train students' Chinese ability, etc. There are more than 50 mini games included in the series of applications. Different abilities of Chinese are tested or trained through the mini games, such as reading, writing and speaking. All game results are recorded for further analysis.
