Road to Graduation

ROAD TO GRADUATION is a PC 2D platformer simulation game built by Unity for users to experience all stages of university life.

Introduction

Road to Graduation is a university simulation game, which players are one of the students in the university. It aims to provide gamified learning for students to encourage learning in a more interesting, engaging and effective way. Throughout the course of the game, players need to attempt quizzes, combat with monsters, learn skills and raise all kinds of abilities to survive in different stages of university life.

Technical Components

Engine

Unity with C# language is used to build a PC game.

Third Person Controls

Controlling the movement of characters and launching skills through different keyboard inputs. These actions have smooth animations created by sprite sheets so as to provide better aesthetics.

Monsters with AI

Monsters with auto pathfinding system have been implemented to combat with players. Different distractions and obstacles have also been designed to slow down the movement of players or block their paths.

Quizzes

Quiz interface has been incorporated for players to answer knowledge-based questions, so as to unlock different skills, get ability points and trigger different storylines.

Inventory & Equipment

Inventory system and equipment closet are constructed for storing and equipping with various items. There is also a shop for buying new items.

Skills & Abilities

Combats skills have been added such that players could engage into more captivating battles. Furthermore, players could increase different types of abilities like defense and speed to enhance their survivability.

Game Details

  1. Talk with Non-player Character (NPC) and answer quizzes

  2. Use ability points to enhance abilities, engage into battles with monsters and prevent getting hindered by distractions

  3. Participate in internship program, satisfy the requirements of client and create your own storyline as different answers will result in different outcomes

  4. Complete the Final Year Project (FYP) by locating necessary resources and defeating the boss professor
